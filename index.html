About

A bit about me

I'm a senior software engineer with over a decade of experience building data platforms that process billions of records. At Nielsen, I led an on-prem-to-AWS migration, cut pipeline runtimes by 93%, and saved nearly $900K a year. At Warner Music Group, I built the ETL systems behind royalty calculations for millions of daily music plays.

Since January 2025, I've been in exploration mode — intentionally stepping outside the corporate track to go deeper into the things I'm most curious about: AI integration, agentic workflows, and building products end-to-end. I shipped a full-stack caregiver coordination app with Claude API integration, 128 tests, and CI pipelines. I'm not on a break — I'm building with more freedom than I've ever had.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new places, diving into a good book, or experimenting with fashion. This site is a living document of where I've been, what I'm building, and where I'm headed.
